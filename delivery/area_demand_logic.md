# Delivery Area Demand Logic: Shin-Maruko Node
Sundubu Tanaka OS v0.2.14

## The Arbitrage: Low Overhead x High Density
- **Base Node:** Shin-Maruko (Low rent, existing kitchen).
- **Demand Node:** Musashi-Kosugi Tower Mansions (10k+ high-LTV households).
- **Advantage:** Vertical density allows for high delivery efficiency and volume.

## Product/Market Fit: Why Sundubu?
- **Thermal Retention:** Soup retains heat 40% longer than solid meals.
- **Complexity:** Hard to make at home (requires special stock/ingredients).
- **Conversion Rate:** Targetting 0.25% of local household daily = 25 bowls/day.

## Performance Forecast
- **Break-even:** 12 bowls/day.
- **Success:** 25 bowls/day (Realistic).
- **Peak:** 40 bowls/day (Market dominance).
