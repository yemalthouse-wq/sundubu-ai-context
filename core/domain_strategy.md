# Domain Strategy
## 3km Cellular Expansion Model

Sundubu Tanaka expands using a cellular territory model.

Each store operates within an exclusive 3km delivery domain.

This domain is treated as an economic cell.

---

## Principle: No Overlap

No new store may open within an existing 3km domain.

This rule exists to protect the economic viability of each store.

The brand grows by expanding into new cells, not by stacking stores in the same territory.

---

## Franchisee Protection

The headquarters commits to protecting franchise profitability.

Store expansion will never sacrifice the revenue of an existing franchisee.

Growth must come from new markets, not internal competition.

---

## Economic Logic

A 3km radius in dense Japanese cities typically contains:

- 50,000 – 200,000 residents
- large office populations
- stable delivery demand

This radius is sufficient to support a single Sundubu Tanaka operation.

---

## Cellular Expansion

Each store becomes a node in a larger urban network.

Example:

Nakameguro Domain  
Shibuya Domain  
Gotanda Domain  
Shirokane Domain  

Each domain functions independently while sharing the same supply chain and operating system.

---

## Strategic Advantage

The 3km rule creates:

- predictable revenue
- strong franchise trust
- premium pricing stability
- sustainable long-term expansion
