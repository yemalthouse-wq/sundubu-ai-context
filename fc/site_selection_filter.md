# Site Selection Filter
## Sundubu Tanaka Store Criteria

This document defines the minimum conditions for opening a new store.

A location must satisfy these conditions to protect the unit economics.

---

## Rent Rule

Maximum rent ratio:

10% of expected revenue.

Example:

Target revenue  
3,000,000 yen

Maximum rent  
300,000 yen

Preferred range  
150,000 – 300,000 yen

---

## Store Size

6 – 10 tsubo

Delivery oriented format.

---

## Delivery Platform Coverage

Location must be inside active delivery zones:

- Uber Eats
- Wolt
- 出前館

---

## Rider Accessibility

Riders must be able to access the store easily.

Preferred conditions:

- street level
- clear entrance
- short pickup time

---

## Kitchen Feasibility

The location must support:

- gas line
- ventilation
- refrigeration

200V electricity preferred but optional.

---

## Domain Integrity

The new store must not violate the 3km domain rule.

Existing franchise territories must be respected.

---

## Final Principle

If the rent or structure breaks the unit economics,
the location must be rejected.

No exceptions.
