# Tokyo Domain Map
## Sundubu Tanaka Urban Deployment

Each store operates a domain with a **3km maximum radius**.

Economic structure:

Core zone: 1.5 – 2 km  
Extended zone: 2 – 3 km

---

## Domain Nodes

### Nakameguro (Base)

Brand origin and operational laboratory.

Target:
- Nakameguro
- Daikanyama
- Ebisu
- Meguro

---

High-density office and trend districts.
