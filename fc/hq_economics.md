# HQ Economics
## Sundubu Tanaka Franchise Model

The headquarters generates revenue primarily through supply chain operations.

---

## Revenue Streams

1. Soup base supply

Franchise stores purchase standardized soup base from HQ.

Expected supply margin:

15 – 25%

---

2. Logistics

Frozen soup kits distributed through centralized logistics.

Logistics margin:

5 – 10%

---

3. Initial franchise fee

One-time onboarding fee.

Expected range:

1,000,000 yen

---

4. Optional services

Brand assets  
menu development  
training support

---

## Strategic Principle

The headquarters does **not depend on royalty extraction**.

Profit should come from **real supply and logistics value**, not from taxing franchise revenue.

---
